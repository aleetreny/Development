# The Infinite Scroll

**Distance is relative. Until now.**

A single-page web experiment where digital depth matches physical reality 1:1.
If the counter says **1.0000m**, your finger has traveled exactly **one meter** on your mouse wheel or trackpad.

### How to Run
1. Download `index.html`.
2. Open it in any web browser.
3. Scroll.

### The Challenge
The world is deeper than you think.
*There are secrets for those who travel far... and for those who try to find their way back.*
